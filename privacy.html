Horo policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Horo is a pre-launch iOS app concept for playful pet horoscopes and personality readings. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Horo landing page collects and how it is used for waitlist and app idea validation.

Information We Collect

When you use the quiz or join the waitlist, we may collect your email address, optional Instagram handle, marketing consent, pet quiz answers, generated result type, lead score, lead quality, UTM parameters, referrer, landing page URL, user agent, device details, and funnel analytics events such as page views, CTA clicks, quiz starts, quiz progress, quiz completions, result views, submit attempts, successful submissions, errors, copy actions, and share clicks.

How We Use Information

We use this information to validate interest in Horo, understand which traffic sources and quiz steps perform best, manage the private iOS waitlist, and contact you about Horo early access and launch updates if you consented.

No Email Selling Or Autoresponder

The landing page does not send automated result emails, transactional emails, confirmation links, or autoresponder messages. It only collects waitlist and survey information.

Where Data Is Stored

Submissions and analytics events are sent to a Google Apps Script Web App connected to a Google Sheet controlled by the Horo owner. If a submission fails, the page may temporarily store pending data in your browser local storage so it can retry later.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by Google services used to store the Google Sheet and Apps Script endpoint.

Your Choices

You can choose not to submit the waitlist form. If you want your information removed from the Horo waitlist, contact us at vectorjarvis@gmail.com.

Entertainment Only

Horo is for entertainment. It does not provide veterinary, medical, behavioral, or professional advice.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to vectorjarvis@gmail.com.
